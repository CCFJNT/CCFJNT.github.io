About SPC

Church of Christ Founded in Jerusalem New Testament San Pablo Local Church (CCFJNT SPC) is a local congregation of the Church of Christ Founded in Jerusalem New Testament (CCFJNT). Our mission is to spread the teachings of Jesus Christ and serve our community with love and compassion. CCFJNT SPC was established in San Pablo City to cater to the spiritual needs of the local community. Over the years, we have grown in faith and numbers, becoming a beacon of hope and faith in the area.


Church Activities

  1. Weekly Bible Study - Every Thursday at 5:00 PM
  2. Prayer Meetings - Quarterly - Thursday at 5:00 PM
  3. Memory Verse every Sunday and Thursday
  4. Youth Fellowship Events

    Members

    Pastors

    Our pastors are dedicated to guiding our congregation in faith and service. They provide spiritual leadership, counseling, and support to our members.

    Christian Youth Action (CYA)

    The Christian Youth Action (CYA) is a vibrant ministry within our church that focuses on nurturing the spiritual growth and leadership skills of our youth. Through various activities, events, and service projects, CYA provides a supportive environment for young members to develop their faith and make a positive impact in their communities.
